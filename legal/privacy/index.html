Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 16, 2026

World Explorer collects account and billing data needed to provide subscriptions and trial access. This page summarizes what data is used and how.

Data We Store

  • Firebase Authentication identity (UID, email, provider metadata).
  • Plan state in Firestore, including trial end date, plan tier, and entitlement flags.
  • Stripe customer/subscription identifiers for billing management.
  • Optional in-app cloud data when enabled by plan (for example, synced challenge/saves).

Billing Data

Payments are processed by Stripe. Card details are handled by Stripe and are not stored by World Explorer servers.

How We Use Data

  • Authenticate users and keep sessions active.
  • Grant and enforce trial/subscription entitlements.
  • Provide customer billing portal access.
  • Operate and improve service reliability.

Data Sharing

Data is shared only with service providers required to operate the app (Firebase and Stripe) or where legally required.

Data Retention

Account plan records are retained while your account remains active. You may request account deletion by contacting support.

Contact

For privacy requests, email hello@worldexplorer.app.
